Our Service

In-Person or Virtual Consultation

Assisting clients in realizing their vision with expert guidance. The Furni design team offers advice on layout planning, color palettes, and product recommendations tailored to the client's style and budget.

Bespoke & Custom Furniture (Customization)

We offer full custom manufacturing, allowing you to tailor size, material, fabric, and finish to your exact needs. This service is ideal for clients seeking truly unique pieces or those requiring specific dimensions for challenging spaces. It emphasizes quality and personalization over mass production.

Insured Delivery & Expert Installation

Enjoy a completely hassle-free experience with our end-to-end logistics. We provide fully insured, secure delivery straight to your location. Our team of highly trained technicians will then perform professional installation, ensuring every piece is assembled correctly and ready for immediate use, with no effort required on your part.

Long-Term Structural Warranty

Furni stands by the quality of its craftsmanship. We offer an extended 5-Year Structural Warranty against defects in the primary framework (frames, bases, and core mechanisms) of our signature furniture pieces. This commitment provides peace of mind, affirming your investment in long-lasting quality

Commercial & Project Sourcing

We act as reliable partners for commercial projects, including offices, hotels, and architectural developments. This service provides bulk pricing, dedicated project consultation, and the ability to fulfill high-volume orders with full customization capabilities, meeting the rigorous standards of corporate design.

Flexible Returns and Dedicated Support

We stand behind our designs and your satisfaction is our priority. Furni offers a 30-Day Happiness Guarantee, providing flexible options for returns or exchanges if the piece doesn't perfectly fit your space or vision. Furthermore, every purchase includes access to a dedicated technical support line for quick assistance regarding assembly, function, or material care.
